Connect 4 Emojis

Connect-Four-Emojis is a two-player game
in which players alternately place Emojis on a vertical board 7 columns across and 6 rows high.
Each player uses Emojis of a particular face (commonly lovely and laugh),
and the object is to be the first to obtain four Emojis in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line.
Because the board is vertical, Emojis inserted in a given column always drop to the lowest unoccupied row of that column.
As soon as a column contains 6 Emojis, it is full and no other Emoji can be placed in the column.
Both players begin with 21 identical Emojis,
and the first player to achieve a line of four connected Emojis wins the game.
If all 42 move are played and no player has places four Emojis in a row, the game is drawn.
